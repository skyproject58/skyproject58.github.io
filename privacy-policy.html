Privacy Policy

Introduction

StealthLink, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Device information, such as unique device identifiers (e.g., READ_PHONE_STATE, AD_ID).
  • Usage Data: Interaction with notifications (via OneSignal) and analytics data (via Firebase).
  • Other Permissions: Vibration feedback, foreground service management, and Bluetooth access.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Providing VPN services and maintaining a stable connection.
  • Sending notifications through OneSignal.
  • Analyzing app performance using Firebase Analytics.
  • Complying with legal obligations or enforcing our Terms of Service.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share data with third-party services to improve functionality:

  • Firebase: For analytics and crash reporting. Privacy Policy.
  • OneSignal: For managing push notifications. Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions

The app requires certain permissions for functionality:

  • Internet & Network Permissions: For VPN connections.
  • Notification Permission: To display alerts and updates.
  • Device State: To identify your device for crash reporting.
  • Bluetooth & Vibration: To support optional features and user feedback.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee complete security, and we advise users to take additional precautions when online.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access and Correction: Request access to your data and correct inaccuracies.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your data.
  • Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads through your device settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the app.
